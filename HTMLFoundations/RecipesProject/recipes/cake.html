Better Than Sex Cake with German Chocolate Cake Mix

Description

This better-than-sex cake recipe is made with German chocolate cake mix, hot fudge dessert topping, and chocolate-covered English toffee. It's crazy good!

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package German chocolate cake mix
  • 1 ¼ cups water
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 3 Large eggs
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cups hot fudge topping
  • 1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 4 (1.4 ounce) bars English toffee-flavored candy, crushed

Directions

Step One

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Grease a 9x13-inch glass baking dish.

Step Two

  1. Mix cake mix, water, oil, and eggs in a large bowl with an electric mixer on medium speed until well combined
  2. Pour batter into the prepared baking dish.

Step Three

  1. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, 30 to 35 minutes
  2. Remove from the oven and set the baking dish on a wire rack.

Step Four

  1. While the cake is still very warm, poke holes all over the top with the end of a wooden spoon
  2. Pour condensed milk over cake and let cool, 20 to 30 minutes.

Step Five

  1. Pour hot fudge topping over cooled cake and let set, 20 to 30 minutes
  2. Spread whipped topping over top and sprinkle with crushed toffee
  3. Bon Appetit
