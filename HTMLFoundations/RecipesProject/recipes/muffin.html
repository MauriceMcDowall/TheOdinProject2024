Bran Muffins a la Brian

Description

A bran muffin made with buttermilk and golden raisins. This is a recipe that is a combination of a variety of trials and errors. It is the only bran muffin recipe my husband likes.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups wheat bran
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon molasses
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup golden raisins

Steps

Step one

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Grease a 12 cup muffin tin, or line with paper muffin liners
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the wheat bran and buttermilk
  4. Set aside to soak until thickened, about 10 minutes.

Step Two

  1. In a large bowl, use an electric mixer to blend together the brown sugar, vegetable oil, egg and molasses
  2. Stir in the bran mixture
  3. Combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt; stir into the batter until just moistened
  4. Mix in raisins
  5. Spoon batter into muffin cups, filling 3/4 full

Step Three

  1. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes in the preheated oven, until the tops spring back when lightly pressed.
